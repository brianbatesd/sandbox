Setting up online bill payments through your bank or credit card? Here's what you'll need to know about our mailing address for Utility bill payments so we receive your payments properly. Please keep in mind that instructions on setting up and editing accounts may vary depending upon your bank or credit card, so it's best to follow the instructions that they provide.

In general, you’ll need to:

  1. Sign into or access your bank or credit card where you have accounts set up for easy bill payments.
  2. In the easy bill pay section, use this address for City of Vancouver Utility payments:

P.O. Box 35195
Seattle, WA 98124-5195

Please note: The P.O. Box above is only our bill mailing address. Our Utility Customer Service office location is located at 2323 General Anderson Rd in Vancouver, Washington. You can reach a City of Vancouver Utility customer service representative during regular business hours by phone at 360-487-7999; by email at utilitiescs@cityofvancouver.us; or in person at the Vancouver address above.

Please note: If you receive paper utility bills in the mail, you can use the accompanying envelope to mail your payment. If you pay your bill online or if you have set up AutoPay to pay your utility bills, you will not need to worry about including the mailing address.

Why a Seattle P.O. Box for payments?

The local payment processor used by the City of Vancouver since the mid-1990s discontinued its service. After exploring and evaluating options used by other governmental entities and businesses in our area, the City entered into a contract with a Seattle-based company to provide secure, cost-effective service with increased efficiencies, including state-of-the-art technology and same-day processing within 24 hours of receipt. That means payments can be applied to customer accounts one day faster. P.O. Box 35195, Seattle, WA 98124-5195, has been set up specifically for Utility bill payments.
