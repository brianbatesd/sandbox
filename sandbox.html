We are the City of Vancouver’s storytellers.

Our award-winning communications team works to provide authentic and proactive communications to help all residents feel well-informed and engaged with the work of their local government. From community outreach to digital marketing, graphic design to video production – we are proud to share the stories that make Vancouver an exceptional place to live, work and play.
