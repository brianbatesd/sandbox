About Vancouver

Vancouver is a full-service city and one of the fastest growing in the state of Washington. With a population of 197,600 Vancouver is the fourth largest city in Washington. The City provides municipal services to 290,000 throughout the urban area.

Vancouver is located along the shores of the Columbia River, 100 miles inland from the Pacific Ocean. The community is defined by its proximity to the river. Today, Vancouver is landing on “best of lists” as a place to live, for its public projects, and amenities that includes its riverfront development.

Vancouver has a vibrant culture, which includes ample outdoor recreation, a thriving arts and music scene, delicious restaurants and craft brewing, boutique shopping, and more. Learn more about what makes Vancouver special at Visit Vancouver WA.

Our Work

The City’s work is guided by three universal themes—Community Safety, Climate Action, and Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion.

Our culture: Vision and Values

Vision: Vancouver is building the riverfront city of the future through our commitment to equity, stewardship, resilience, and community safety.

 If our vision and values align with yours, then join our team!

Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion at the City

Starting in February 2018, the City of Vancouver has been intentionally working to improve and advance diversity, equity, and inclusion (DEI) of the communities it serves and the employees who work at the City. The City believes each employee makes an important contribution to its operations and the community with talents and expertise that are unique and valuable.

Equal Opportunity Employer

The City of Vancouver is committed to providing equal employment opportunities to all individuals. How To Apply

For a successful application process, please follow these simple guidelines:

  • Record your username and password when you set up an account. You will need it to apply for other positions or to check the status of your application. When you re-enter the system, you will need to input this information exactly as it was originally entered.
  • Submit a complete application on time. The closing date and time are noted on the job posting. Late applications will not be accepted.
  • Include all relevant education and experience that you have when applying for a position. If you held more than one job with an employer, list each job separately.
  • Click "Submit" before the application deadline!
  • Check your email (including your spam folder) and voicemail for job related communications, invitations to interview and updates to your status on a regular basis.
  • Additional general applicant information.

Online applications are stored on a secure site. Only authorized City employees have access to the information submitted.
