Mulching and Weeding at The Downs

Get ready to break a sweat and restore some soils at The Downs Neighborhood Park! The Naturespaces Program is going to be spreading wood chips at The Downs. Adding wood chips to the landscape bed will support this park's health all year long, even during hot summers, and provide park neighbors with new flowers and greenery to enjoy.

Later in the day, visit us at the Downs Park for Art in the Park with Fourth Plain Forward.

Gloves, water, and snacks will be provided. Please bring a reusable water bottle and your own gloves or tools if you would like to. Long pants and closed-toed shoes are required.

Youth under 18 must bring a signed minor consent form.
