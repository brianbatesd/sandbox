Attention: Limited Building Inspection Availability

Due to mandatory training, the City of Vancouver will have limited Building Inspection coverage on Monday and Tuesday, May 8 and 9. Building Inspections scheduled for these days may be rescheduled for another date.

Please limit inspection requests.

We apologize for the inconvenience and thank you for your patience.

City building and electrical inspectors conduct inspections to check for compliance with approved plans, in addition to local and State building code regulations.

Inspection Scheduling User's Guide and Inspection Codes (PDF)

Customers can use their computer or mobile web device to access our web site to:

  • Schedule an Inspection
  • Cancel a scheduled inspection
  • Leave a detailed message for their inspector
  • Retrieve inspection results and history
  • Verify permit status

The system is available 7 days a week from 5 a.m. to 10 p.m.

Inspections received after 6:30 a.m. will be inspected the next business day.

Customers can also use their telephone to schedule an inspection and leave a voice message for their inspector by calling 360-487-7890.

Tip: Always remember to get a confirmation number to ensure that your inspection was scheduled.
