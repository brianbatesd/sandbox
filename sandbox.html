In accordance with the Open Public Meetings Act (OPMA), the City/County Telecommunications Commission meeting will be open to in-person attendance. Options for viewing and/or participating in the meeting remotely will also be accommodated (see details below).

The regular meeting of the Commission will begin at 3:30 p.m. and be televised LIVE on CVTV Cable Channel 23, 323 HD and www.cvtv.org.

Testimony will be accepted in the following manner:

  • Written comments submitted in advance - Comments may be sent to tracie.ramirez@cityofvancouver.us by 12:00 p.m. on March 1. Comments will be compiled and sent to the Telecommunications Commission and entered into the record.
  • In-person or remote testimony during the meeting - Advance registration is required to testify. Anyone wishing to testify must register by 12:00 p.m. on March 1. Call 360-487-8712 or email tracie.ramirez@cityofvancouver to register.

Further instructions for remotely accessing the meeting will be provided upon registration. Each person will be asked to limit testimony to three minutes and provide their name and city of residence for the record.
