Plan Implementation

On August 17, 2020, the City Council adopted the Heights District Plan and directed staff to move forward with initial steps to implement the Plan. The first stage of Heights District Plan implementation includes the creation of a new zoning district and associated design guidelines, and the adoption of a Planned Action for the District. The new zoning code and design guidelines will require their own Planning Commission and City Council adoption process, including workshops and hearings, which will include opportunities for public feedback and comment. Heights District Plan: Outreach and Engagement Overview

The Heights District community planning process was designed to engage a diverse group of neighborhood and community stakeholders. The project team committed to an open and transparent process, and used a variety of methods to ensure that all stakeholders were able to meaningfully participate. This included one-on-one meetings, focus groups, stakeholder interviews, community open houses, online surveys, and targeted outreach to underrepresented populations. In addition to a Community Advisory Committee that met regularly throughout the planning process, city and consultant staff met regularly with neighborhood associations, small businesses, property owners, service providers and public agencies operating in the district.

The following objectives guided public outreach and engagement activities undertaken as part of this planning process:

  • Engage people with diverse experiences and views
  • Be inclusive and transparent
  • Ensure stakeholders are well informed
  • Reflect and acknowledge community input in decisions
  • Strengthen the community and empower participants
  • Engage the private sector

Community Advisory Committee

The Community Advisory Committee (CAC) met regularly throughout the duration of the project and was comprised of representatives from neighborhood associations, community organizations, health and social service providers, businesses, government agencies, and individuals from the community (roster). Project stakeholders will receive periodic updates and information about how they can continue to participate in implementation of the Heights District Plan over time. These include neighborhood associations and coalitions, the Community Advisory Committee, agency partners, property owners, small businesses, service providers and school groups.

CAC meeting materials are available to download on the Project documents and links page.
