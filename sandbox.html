2. L & I ACTIVITY PRESCRIPTION FORM (APF)

This form is to be completed by the treating medical provider. The employee shall submit this completed form to his/her supervisor as soon as possible after the appointment. An employee must be authorized time off in order to be eligible for a time loss payment.

3. RxBRIDGE FIRST FILL PRESCRIPTION CARD

Employee shall present this form to their pharmacist for any prescription(s) required to treat their workplace injury/illness. Employee can call RxBridge ahead of time and provide the required information. Employee will now be in the pharmacy’s system so there will be no charge for the prescription(s).
