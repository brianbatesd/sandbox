Join City staff from Naturespaces, Volunteer Programs and Urban Forestry to steward one of our beautiful Urban Natural Areas, Countryside Woods Neighborhood Park! This event will take place rain or shine from 9 a.m. to 11:30 a.m. Saturday, July 15, 2023.

We will be working on getting pesky invasive blackberry out of the forest understory and spreading mulch to suppress the regrowth of invasive plants.

Our efforts at this site are all working towards the goal of restoring the health of the forest ecosystem at Countryside Park. Join us in creating a haven for pollinators, wildlife and people!

Beverages, water, snacks, gloves and tools will be provided. Dress for the weather and wear long pants and closed toed shoes that can get damp or dirty. If you have a wheelbarrow, digging fork, pitch fork, garden shovel or hand shovel please feel welcome to bring it along.

Youth under 18 must bring a signed minor consent form.
