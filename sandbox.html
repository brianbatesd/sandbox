Join the John Ball Adopt-A-Park group and City staff in stewarding this beautiful community park! This event will take place rain or shine from 10 a.m. to 12 p.m. Saturday, July, 29, 2023.

We will be working on a variety of projects including cleaning up the playground and signs, pulling invasive plants, removing litter, and mulching.

Water, snacks, gloves and tools will be provided. Dress for the weather and wear long pants and closed toed shoes that can get damp or dirty. If you have a wheelbarrow, digging fork, pitch fork, garden shovel or hand shovel please feel welcome to bring them along.

Youth under 18 must bring a signed minor consent form.

Advance registration is required. RSVP here.
