Visit MyCoVBenefits.com for comprehensive benefit options, overviews, rates, plan documents and more resource information. The list below is just an overview of benefits available to most.

Please note: Benefits often differ by employee group or bargaining unit. In addition, benefits are normally pro-rated for employees working less than full-time. The Benefit Overviews by employee group and union contracts are a great place to see which benefits you are eligible for.

If you are separating from service, be sure to closely review the Separations and Retirements page to get prepared.

Most benefit elections are self-service through Workday (the City of Vancouver Human Resources Information System); you may find job aids to help you with enrollments and changes to your benefits on the benefits website: Workday Job Aids.

Complete Insurance Coverage

Medical/Rx, Dental, Vision: A variety of plans offered through:

  • Regence BlueCross BlueShield of Oregon,
  • Kaiser Permanente Northwest,
  • Kaiser Permanente Dental Northwest,
  • Delta Dental of Washington, and
  • Vision Services Plan (VSP).

Generous Paid Leaves

Examples include Personal Time Off, Vacation, Exempt Leave, Holidays, Sick Leave & Bereavement.

Flexible Spending Account

Our Flexible Spending Accounts (FSA) are an excellent way to save on taxes and boost your benefits, we offer both medical and daycare/dependent FSAs. The Medical FSA plan can be used for expenses related to medical, dental, vision prescription, and other qualified expenses while the Daycare FSA can be used for daycare/dependent care and other qualified expenses.

DRS Retirement and Deferred Compensation

Most employees will participate in a State of Washington Department of Retirement Systems (DRS) Plan where you and the City of Vancouver contribute towards your retirement. If eligible, depending on your position, you will participate in the Public Employees Retirement System (PERS) Plan 2 or 3 or the Law Enforcement Officers’ and Fire Fighters’ Retirement System (LEOFF) Plan 2.

Employees may also choose to participate in a 457(b) Deferred Compensation Plan through Mission Square Retirement. Employees in the Police Guild, Fire Suppression and Fire Command may also participate in WA DRS 457(b) Deferred Compensation Program in addition to Mission Square. Review your Benefit Overview by employee group to see if you are eligible for an employer match.

Long-Term Disability (LTD) Insurance

Most employees will have an income protection benefit in the form of Long-Term Disability offered through New York Life Insurance.

Life and Accidental Death & Dismemberment (AD&D) Insurance

Most employees will have Life and AD&D coverage through New York Life with the option to purchase additional coverage for yourself and your dependents.

Employee Assistance Program (EAP)

A free and confidential benefit that can assist employees and their dependents with problems that interfere with day-to-day activities. Includes a personal wellness component. This benefit is offered through Canopy and includes discounts through LifeBalance.

Additional Benefits

We offer additional benefits such as Aflac, 529 college savings, and pre-paid legal plan.

Commute Trip Reduction

Most employees are eligible to participate in the Commute Trip Reduction Program and receive either a free bus pass or monthly stipend.

In the event of an error or conflict of information, the actual Plan document or Summary Plan Description shall be considered correct.
