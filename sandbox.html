Local Maps

  • Vancouver/Clark County Bike Map (PDF): Copies of the City's bike map are available at Vancouver City Hall, located at 415 W 6th Street, which is open from 8 a.m. to 5 p.m., Monday through Friday.
    • Please note: Bicycling is not allowed on Ridgefield Refuge trails; these trails are for pedestrians only.
  • Vancouver/Clark County Bike Map (interactive web version): Created by Clark County GIS, this interactive, online version of the bike map includes all of Clark County and Portland.
  • Vancouver Parks and Recreation Trail Map (PDF): Maps of all trails in Clark County, as well as information on regional parks.
  • I-5 Bridge Map (PDF): A detailed bicycle map of the I-5 bridge crossing that shows cyclists how to access the I-5 bridge in downtown Vancouver, cross the I-5 bridge, and travel through Jantzen Beach and Delta Park toward downtown Portland.
  • City of Portland Bike Maps: A collection of maps for the City of Portland bicycle network.
  • Metro Bike There Map: A bicycle map of the Portland metropolitan area - covers from Troutdale to Forest Grove, Wilsonville to Vancouver.

Regional Maps

  • Washington State Bike Map: Information on bicycling around Washington State.
  • Oregon State Bike Map: Information on bicycling around Oregon State.
  • Columbia River Gorge Bike Map: Information on bicycle routes through the Columbia Gorge.
  • Forty Mile Loop Trail: A 40-Mile bicycling and hiking trail loop in the Portland Metro area.
  • Oregon Coast Bike Route Map: Information on bicycle routes along the Oregon coast.
